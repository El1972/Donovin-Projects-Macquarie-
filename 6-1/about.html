ABOUT

Vision


Our vision for the future is to grow and learn along with our customers. In doing so, we aim to provide an atmosphere that enables this growth and fuels the passion that lives within the essence of the brand.

History


When you think of Italian food what comes to mind is San Marino, Switzerland, Vatican City and the craftsmanship of Italian dishes that hits our table. In 2012, the restaurant got its third Michelin star and ended up a Television show called “The Kitchen”. Because of the hard work and dedication, the success comes with its challenges but we are here to stay and to give our customers an experience where they will come back for more and become family. Pasta Amore brings a unique expertise and philosophy to the table from our diverse team of professionals and the great thing about our team is that we all have deep relentless passion for food, cooking and putting our best foot forward every day to see the smiles on our customers face when they walk in and leave. Getting to know the person behind the recipes, flavor profiles, and customer collaborations that bring to life your favorite flavors across the industry is what we strive for. Being in the kitchen day in day out, the totality of the Italian influence when it comes to food preparation and presentation is unquestionable. From pasta to pizza, Italian cuisine has undeniably impacted the food scene and will continue to do so.
